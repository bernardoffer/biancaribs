Water quality and Fertirrigation

Water quality plays a crucial role in fertigation, which is the practice of applying fertilizers through irrigation water. The quality of water used for fertigation can impact plant health, nutrient availability, and overall crop productivity. Factors that influence crop yield: Water Source, pH Levels, Electrical Conductivity (EC), others.
